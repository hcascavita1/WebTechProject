About Me

I find it a bit difficult when people ask me to introduce myself. You might ask 'why?' my simple answer is that I'm from a lot of places. I usually start by saying that I was born in Miami, US moved to Venezuela lived there for 8 years then moved back to Miami and my parents are from Colombia. I also tell them that it's up to them to derive where I'm from but I consider myself Colombian.

My dad did his graduate program in Boston College and both my uncles studied at Boston University. Every since I was little I always dreamed of going to school in Boston because I wanted to follow my dad's footsteps. As I grew up I realized that my passion was business and entrepreneurship and luckily for me Babson College is one of the best business programs in the country.
