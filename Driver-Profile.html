2023 Forumula 1 Drivers' Profile



Max Verstappen

  • DOB: September 30, 1997
  • From: Hasselt, Belgium
  • Current Team: Red Bull Racing
  • Wins: 37
  • Podiums: 80
  • Facts: Verstappen became the youngest driver to compete in Formula One at the age of 17. He won his first race in 2016 at the Spanish Grand Prix, becoming the youngest driver to win a race in F1. He also holds the record for the most career podiums before winning his first championship (22).
